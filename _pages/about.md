## About Me

I am a Ph.D student in Biostatistics at the [University of Washington](https://www.biostat.washington.edu/). My research focuses on developing statistical and machine learning methods for large-scale genomic studies and public health studies.

Previously, I obtained my MS in Biostatistics from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2024.

[[UW Student Page]](https://www.biostat.washington.edu/people/shiyu-wan)
